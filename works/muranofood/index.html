Murano Food

Murano Food is the intranet service for ordering lunches from selected restaurants. Every Monday, employees order lunches for weeks with money have gotten from employers.
Prototype→
The previous version works badly and was useless. Appearance doesn’t associate with food.
I conduct interviews with users, collect problems and prioritize them.
In the new version, instead of the calendar, there is sidebar with order for the week. Restaurants are now visible and you can switch between them with a single click.
The ingredients and nutrition facts are displayed in the dialogue.
A special algorithm collects the restaurant menu from the dish cards. It looks at the available information for the dish and the nearest cards and makes a decision based on this.
